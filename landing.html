About Retrieve

Retrieve is a modern solution to an age-old problem. Our platform seamlessly connects people who have lost items with those who have found them, fostering a community of trust and support.

How It Works

Post details about your lost or found item securely. Our system catalogues the entry, and you can wait for administrative approval to finalize the match safely and transparently.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an item?

Click on the "Report Lost Item" or "Report Found Item" button on your dashboard to securely fill out the necessary details.

What happens after I submit a report?

Your report is securely sent to an administrator for review. Once verified, it will be visible on the public feed to help facilitate a match.

Privacy Policy
